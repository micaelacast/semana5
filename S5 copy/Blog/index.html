Five Easy Ways To Design Your Ideal Room

The best way to fulfill the room of your dreams is by planning it first. Perfect designs come to life with trial and error. Trying what looks best before you set gives you a better look on the amount of potential styles your room could have. This is why designers work with a huge amount of requirements before assembling their design. And that's also why they aren't cheap! We talked to many of our insider interior designers and came up with easy ways to help you bring to life the room of your dreams all by yourself.
Often starting with the help of mood boards, designers can narrow down the elements of their design. They can divide their mood boards by color palette, textures, furniture pieces, spaces, measurements, etc.

Why You Should Read The Bohemians

Written by Justina Blackeney, the architectural and interior design based book has turned into a best seller. With it's amazing colors and textures waiting to inspire you to turn your home into a bohemian hut, Blackeney shares DIYs and the best furniture combinations. The book is filled with the history of furniture pieces and the origin of the bohemian style. It answers your questions such as, "Why is this style so popular right now?." The Bohemians makes the boho style look so complicated. However, it is quite effortless. It basically joins every furniture piece that you wouldn't think would go together.
